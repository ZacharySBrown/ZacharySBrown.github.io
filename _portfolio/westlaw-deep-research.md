Led the design, development, and launch of Westlaw Advantage Deep Research - the first agentic legal research system deployed to market. This system uses multi-agent orchestration to conduct comprehensive legal research, synthesizing information from multiple sources and presenting findings in a structured, actionable format.

**Key Achievements:**
* First-to-market agentic legal research system
* Multi-agent RAG architecture for complex query decomposition
* Production deployment serving thousands of legal professionals
* Novel evaluation frameworks for legal research quality
* Built and launched in four months from prototype to product

**Technologies:** LangChain, RAG architectures, Agent orchestration, Prompt engineering, Evaluation frameworks

**Team:** Grew team from 2 to 9 applied research scientists and engineers to support rapid evolution in ML and GenAI products
