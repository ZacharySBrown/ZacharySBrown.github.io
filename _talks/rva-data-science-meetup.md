Founded and organize the Richmond Virginia Data Science Community Meetup, building a community of 1500+ data practitioners across the greater Richmond area. Regular monthly events featuring technical talks, workshops, and networking opportunities.

**Role:** Founding Organizer (2017 - Present)

**Activities:**
* Monthly technical talks from industry practitioners and researchers
* Hands-on workshops on ML, data engineering, and analytics
* Community building and networking events
* Career development and mentorship opportunities

**Impact:**
* 1500+ members across the Richmond area
* 100+ events organized since 2017
* Created a thriving community for data science professionals in Richmond
* Mentored dozens of early-career data scientists
