Adjunct faculty teaching graduate and undergraduate courses in the Decision Analytics program at VCU School of Business. Courses focus on emerging technologies, distributed computing, and practical ML applications in business contexts.

**Role:** Adjunct Professor (2019 - 2021)

**Courses Taught:**

* **DAPT615 - Emerging Technologies:**
  * Cloud computing and distributed systems
  * Modern ML frameworks and applications
  * Practical deployment of ML models
  * Hands-on projects with real-world datasets

* **SCMA645 - Management Science (Teaching Assistant):**
  * Quantitative modeling and optimization
  * Analytics for business decision-making
  * Data-driven problem solving

**Teaching Philosophy:** Emphasize practical, hands-on learning with real-world applications. Bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice by bringing current industry experience into the classroom.
